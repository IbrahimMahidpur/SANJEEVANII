## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**If Sanjeevani voice not working:**
- Check June VA window is running (should show "Listening for sound...")
- Verify microphone permissions in browser
- Click blue button to activate audio

**If any service not responding:**
- Check the service window for errors
- Verify port is listening: `netstat -ano | findstr ":PORT"`
- Restart specific service if needed
